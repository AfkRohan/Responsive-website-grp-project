Welcome to the work of Creative-Based Photographer

Patel photography is a best Photography in London

We always had a creative side whether that's taking photos, playing instruments or designing buildings.

Our photography gives viewers a unique and fresh perspective on cities. In London he has had his photos showcased in the Saatchi Gallery as well as printed in Best of England's Book: 'The London Guide for Instagrammers'. He has gained acclamation from organisations such as Visit London, TimeOut, @London, Secret London, Metro and The Evening Standard.

Globally, we have worked with a number of notable brands (Adobe, Davidoff, Acer) and has ambassador roles for Xiaomi, Samyang, Lume Cube and DataColour. With 100,000 followers on Social Media (Instagram & TikTok), his work is admired and shared by countless numbers of people.

Taking on board his thorough knowledge of London, We enjoys spending our time working with people around the capital - be that through teaching Photography Workshops or taking Photoshoots.
