Photography is the art, science, and technique of creating long-lasting images by capturing light, either chemically or electronically using a light-sensitive substance like photographic film or an image sensor. It is used in numerous industries, including manufacturing (such as photolithography) and business, in addition to its more obvious applications in the arts, film and video production, recreational activities, hobbies, and mass communication.
